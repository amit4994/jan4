About Us

We are living in an age where tasks and systems are fusing together with the power of IOT (internet of things) to have a more efficient system of working and to execute jobs quickly. This is our effort to automate the waste management. The SMART LITTER BIN not only opens up automatically when it detects that someone is out there to throw the garbage but also informs about the garbage level inside it. An innovative way that will help to keep the cities clean and healthy. This will be our small contribution to the Cleanliness Campaign.
